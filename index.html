Company Overview

AR Polymer Industries Ltd. is one of the largest private limited chemical companies in Bangladesh. Continual research and development, state of the art production facility, quality products, competent services, and countrywide customer service have made it warmly acceptable to the customers. AR Polymer Industries Ltd. was incorporated in 2007 as a private limited business enterprise. It is powered by 50 skilled manpower. The relentless efforts and dedication of these people are providing continual help to produce and maintain the proper product quality, just in time delivery, pre and post sales services to maintain a long term business relationship with the customers. To meet countrywide demand of its products and services, AR Polymer Industries Ltd. has extensive distribution network throughout Bangladesh. In an effort to introduce its products globally, AR Polymer Industries Ltd. has established an indenting company name is "AT-TIZARAH INDENTERS".

AR Polymer Industries Ltd. has already experienced its products and service supply to India, Yemen, Ghana, Sudan, Uganda, Nigeria, Saudi Arabia, and United Kingdom. AR Polymer Industries Ltd. enhances the business of its customers by providing them with complete solutions. AR Polymer Industries Ltd. focuses on the customer's demand with appropriate products and solutions as well as services.
